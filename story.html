The Magic Forest

Once upon a time, in a land not so far away, there was a magical forest. This forest was full of talking animals, sparkling streams, and trees that whispered secrets to those who listened carefully.

You have just arrived at the edge of this forest. The path ahead splits into two directions - one leads deeper into the woods, and the other follows a bubbling stream.

The Talking Owl

As you walk along the path, you notice a wise old owl sitting on a branch above you. To your surprise, the owl turns its head and speaks!

"Hello there, young adventurer," hoots the owl. "I've been expecting you. The forest has many wonders to show those who are brave and kind."

The Magic Stone

The owl tells you about a special magic stone hidden somewhere in the forest. This stone has the power to grant one wish to whoever finds it.

"But remember," warns the owl, "the forest will test your courage and kindness before revealing its treasures."
